Tonight’s Study: Psalm 23

  1. Read Psalm 23 slowly one time.
  2. Circle the words that show God’s care: shepherd, leadeth, restoreth, comfort.
  3. Ask: What does this show me about God?
  4. Ask: What does this show me about what my soul needs?
